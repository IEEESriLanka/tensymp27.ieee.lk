Special Invited Papers

Home > Call for Contributions > Special Invited Papers

In addition to regular submissions, the specific technical committees have curated a selection of Special Invited Papers from world-renowned experts.

Overview

Special Invited Papers provide deep insights into state-of-the-art technologies and future directions. These papers are by invitation only, though proposals for special sessions focusing on specific themes are welcome.

Special Session: AI for Social Good

Organizer: Dr. Jane Doe, Tech University

This session explores how Artificial Intelligence can be leveraged to address global challenges such as healthcare accessibility, climate change, and education.

Special Session: Future of Wireless 6G

Organizer: Prof. John Smith, Institute of Communications

A focused look at the enabling technologies for 6G networks, including terahertz communications and intelligent reflecting surfaces.

Propose a Special Session

If you fit the criteria and wish to propose a special session, please contact the program chairs. Your proposal should include:

  • Title of the Special Session
  • Rationale and Scope
  • List of Potential Invited Speakers/Authors
