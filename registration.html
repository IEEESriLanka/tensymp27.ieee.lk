Important Guidelines for Authors of Accepted Papers

  • Each non-student registration allows the presentation of up to two papers.
  • Each student registration allows the presentation of one paper only.
  • Authors should enter the paper details and ID numbers for all papers being presented under a single non-student registration in the registration form.

Registration Details & Deadlines

  • Early Bird Rates apply until 15 March 2027. Fees will increase slightly after this date.
  • TENSYMP 2027 is primarily a physical conference, and authors are expected to present in person.
  • Online presentations will be permitted only in extreme emergency cases, subject to committee approval.

What's Included in Registration

  • Access to all technical sessions, keynote speeches, and workshops
  • Conference proceedings and materials
  • Coffee breaks and lunches during conference days
  • Conference kit and badge
  • Social functions (for author registrations)
  • Certificate of participation
