Call for Tutorials

IEEE TENSYMP 2027 invites proposals for half-day (3 hours) or full-day (6 hours) tutorials on new and emerging topics within the broad scope of Computing and Artificial Intelligence.

Download PDF

Overview

IEEE TENSYMP 2027 invites proposals for half-day (3 hours) or full-day (6 hours) tutorials on new and emerging topics within the broad scope of Computing and Artificial Intelligence. Share your expertise with the community by proposing a focused, technically engaging tutorial session.

Proposals should be submitted as a PDF not exceeding 5 pages, covering objectives, outline, presenter background, and relevance to the symposium theme. Selected tutorials will be scheduled in the second week of April 2027 and featured on the symposium website.


Topics

The topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Data Science and Applications
  • Parallel and Distributed Computing
  • Human-Computer Interaction and Computer Vision
  • Knowledge Management and Information Systems
  • Software Engineering
  • Technology-Enhanced Learning and Teaching
  • Ethical and Sustainable Computing
  • Trustworthy and Explainable AI
  • Generative AI and Societal Impact

Tutorial Proposal Format

Tutorial proposals should be clear, concise, and provide a comprehensive overview of the planned session. Each proposal must include:

  • Abstract, objectives, motivation, and intended audience
  • Names and short biographies of all tutorial presenters
  • Detailed outline of the tutorial, including a tentative schedule
  • Past and relevant expertise of the speaker(s) on the proposed topic
  • Previous tutorial experience of the speaker(s), including past versions (if any)

Selection & Acceptance

All tutorial proposals will be peer-reviewed. The Tutorial Chairs will make a final selection to ensure variety and relevance to the symposium attendees. Speakers for accepted tutorials will be required to sign a commitment to present within 14 days of notification. Tutorial material must be emailed to the organizers at least 14 days before the presentation date.
