Conference Venue

Conference Venue

Faculty of Engineering
University of Peradeniya
Peradeniya 20400
Kandy, Sri Lanka

The Faculty of Engineering at the University of Peradeniya is one of Sri Lanka's premier engineering institutions. Established in 1950, it is set within the largest university campus in Sri Lanka, spanning over 700 hectares of scenic landscape.

The venue offers modern conference facilities, lecture halls, and breakout rooms suitable for technical sessions, workshops, and networking events.

Campus Access

Main Entrance

Enter via the Galaha Road gate. Show your conference badge to security. Visitor parking available near the Engineering Faculty.

Registration Desk

Located at the main foyer of the New Auditorium. Open from 7:30 AM on conference days.

Parking

Free parking available for attendees at the designated conference parking area. Follow signage from the main gate.

Accessibility

Wheelchair access available at all main venues. Contact us in advance for special assistance requirements.
