Travel & Visa Support

Travel & Visa Support

Getting to Sri Lanka

Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB) in Colombo is the main international gateway. The airport is approximately 115 km from Kandy (3–3.5 hours by road).

By Air

Direct flights operate from major Asian hubs including Singapore, Dubai, Delhi, Bangkok, and Kuala Lumpur. SriLankan Airlines is the national carrier with connections worldwide.

Airport to Kandy

Pre-arranged transfers available via the registration portal (LKR 12,000). Alternatively, take an Uber or taxi (~LKR 10,000–15,000) or the scenic train from Colombo Fort (3–4 hours).

Local Transportation

Uber

Available in Kandy area

PickMe

Local ride-hailing app

Conference Shuttle

Free from partner hotels

Tuk-Tuk

LKR 300–500 to venue

Visa Requirements

Most nationalities require an Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) to enter Sri Lanka. The ETA is valid for 30 days and can be extended.

Apply online: www.eta.gov.lk
Processing time: 24–48 hours | Fee: USD 50 (subject to change)

Invitation Letters: If you require a visa support letter for your application, please email visa@tensymp2027.org with your registration confirmation and passport details.

Travel Tips

Currency

Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR). ATMs widely available. Major credit cards accepted at hotels and restaurants. USD 1 ≈ LKR 320.

Weather

May in Kandy: Warm and humid (25–30°C). Occasional rain showers. Pack light clothing and an umbrella.

Power

230V, 50Hz. Type D and Type G plugs. Bring a universal adapter.

Emergency

Police: 119 | Ambulance: 1990 | Tourist Police: 1912
