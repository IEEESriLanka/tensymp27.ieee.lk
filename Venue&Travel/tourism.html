Tourism & Excursions

Tourism

Kandy, a UNESCO World Heritage City, is the cultural capital of Sri Lanka. Nestled among misty hills, it offers a rich blend of history, spirituality, and natural beauty. Take some time to explore these attractions during your visit.

Nearby Attractions

Temple Image

Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic

Sri Lanka's most sacred Buddhist site, housing the tooth relic of Lord Buddha. Located by Kandy Lake.

6 km from venue
Garden Image

Royal Botanic Gardens

One of the finest botanical gardens in Asia, spanning 147 acres with over 4,000 plant species.

2 km from venue
Lake Image

Kandy Lake

Scenic artificial lake in the heart of the city. Perfect for an evening stroll.

6 km from venue
View Image

Bahiravokanda Buddha Statue

Giant white Buddha statue on a hilltop offering panoramic views of Kandy city.

8 km from venue
Tea Image

Tea Plantations

Visit nearby tea estates in Hanthana or take a day trip to Nuwara Eliya tea country.

15–60 km from venue
Dance Image

Kandyan Dance Show

Traditional cultural performances held daily at the Kandy Lake Club and Red Cross Hall.

6 km from venue
