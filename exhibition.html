Exhibition Opportunities

Exhibition Opportunities

The IEEE TENSYMP 2027 exhibition provides an opportunity for companies and organisations to showcase products, services, and research capabilities to conference attendees. The exhibition area will be located adjacent to the main conference halls and refreshment areas, ensuring high visibility and foot traffic.

Standard Booth

$1,500
  • 3m × 2m booth space
  • 1 table and 2 chairs
  • Power outlet (230V)
  • Company name on booth signage
  • 1 exhibitor badge
  • Listing in conference programme

Premium Booth

$2,500
  • 4m × 3m booth space
  • 2 tables and 4 chairs
  • Multiple power outlets
  • Custom booth signage
  • 2 exhibitor badges
  • Logo in conference programme
  • Priority location selection

Corner Booth

$3,500
  • 5m × 4m corner space
  • Open on two sides
  • 3 tables and 6 chairs
  • Dedicated power supply
  • Large custom signage
  • 3 exhibitor badges
  • Prime corner location

Exhibition floor plan will be available after 1 December 2026

Exhibition Details

Exhibition Schedule

  • Setup: 11 May 2027, 14:00 – 18:00
  • Day 1: 12 May 2027, 08:00 – 18:00
  • Day 2: 13 May 2027, 08:00 – 18:00
  • Day 3: 14 May 2027, 08:00 – 15:00
  • Breakdown: 14 May 2027, 15:00 – 18:00

Location

Exhibition Hall, Faculty of Engineering
University of Peradeniya

The exhibition area is located on the ground floor adjacent to the main auditorium and refreshment areas.

What's Included

  • Wi-Fi access throughout exhibition
  • Basic booth lighting
  • Refreshments for exhibitors
  • Security during exhibition hours
  • Basic cleaning services

Additional Services

  • Extra tables and chairs (on request)
  • Audio-visual equipment rental
  • Additional power requirements
  • Storage facilities
  • Shipping and customs assistance
